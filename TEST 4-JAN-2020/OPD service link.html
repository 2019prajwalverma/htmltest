What is OPD?

Hospital services are usually divided into three main categories. OPD , IPD and Emergency. OPD means out patient department.Here the patient visit the outdoor room, consult with the doctor for his/ her problem,take the medicine prescribed by the doctor and go back. But if the doctor feels that the outdoor patient requires admission and needs indoor treatment then the patient is kept in the ward. IPD means indoor patient department.Here the patients are admitted in the ward and treated accordingly. Emergency or casualty means when the patient is treated on urgent basis.A hospital provide emergency services for 24 hours.
